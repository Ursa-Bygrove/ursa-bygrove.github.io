Software Development

We deliver solutions for small and large businesses alike, specialising in the development of high-performance microservices, data-driven APIs, and process automation tools on Unix platforms.

We have decades of experience working with 3rd party APIs, complex database structures, virtualisation software, and cloud systems. Our primary workhorses are Ruby and Docker, but our expertise also includes Crystal, PHP, Perl, JavaScript, BASH, and Python.

We also bring to the table a formidable set of site-reliability skills, which cover everything from application containerisation, to infrastructure provisioning, to continuous integration and deployment tools: Docker, Terraform, Ansible, Github Actions and GitLab CI/CD.

Graphic Design

We understand the importance of visual presentation - just as a book is judged by its cover, so is a business judged by its website.

We provide a range of design services for both digital and print media including dynamic web design, UI/UX, logo, branding, and design of marketing materials.

Our creative solutions are tailor-made to your needs, be it vector graphic or photo manipulation, accompanied by bespoke typography, iconography, and hand-crafted custom lettering.

We work with individuals and businesses of all shapes and sizes to produce great designs using industry-standard software: Sketch, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign.
